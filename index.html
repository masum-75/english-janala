Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also provide structured courses to guide you step by step.

2. Is this website free to use?

Yes, this website is completely free to use. You can access lessons, vocabulary, and practice exercises without any payment.

3. Do I need to create an account?

No, creating an account is not required. However, if you create one, you can track your learning progress and save your activities.

4. How can I build my English vocabulary?

You can build your vocabulary by practicing daily lessons, learning new words with meanings and pronunciation, and using them in sentences.

5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

Currently, we do not offer certificates. However, we are working on adding certification features in the future.
